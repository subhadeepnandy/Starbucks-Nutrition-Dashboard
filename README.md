☕ Starbucks Nutrition Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

The Starbucks Nutrition Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project that analyzes the nutritional information of Starbucks beverages. The dashboard helps users understand calorie intake, sugar levels, caffeine content, protein, fat, sodium, carbohydrates, and other nutritional values across different beverage categories.

The project demonstrates how Business Intelligence can transform raw nutrition data into actionable insights for customers and business stakeholders.

🎯 Business Problem

Customers often find it difficult to compare the nutritional values of Starbucks drinks before purchasing. Similarly, businesses require insights into product categories with higher calories, sugar, or caffeine for better product positioning and healthier menu planning.

This dashboard solves these challenges through interactive visualizations.

💡 Business Insights
Coffee beverages generally have lower calories than blended drinks.
Frappuccino beverages contain significantly higher sugar and calorie levels.
Espresso-based drinks provide high caffeine with comparatively fewer calories.
Smoothies offer higher protein but also contain more carbohydrates.
White Chocolate Mocha and Java Chip Frappuccino are among the highest-calorie beverages.
Beverage preparation size has a direct impact on calorie and caffeine intake.
